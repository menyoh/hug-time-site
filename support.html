What Hug Time does

Hug Time helps you track daily hug goals, mood check-ins, hug missions, streaks, and gentle reminders to connect with someone in a consent-first way.

Common questions

How do I turn reminders off?

Open Hug Time, go to the Adaptive Reminders section, and turn off Daily nudge. You can also disable notifications in iOS Settings.

Why did my streak reset?

Your streak increases when you hit your daily hug goal. If you miss a day, the streak resets. If eligible, the app may offer an optional streak recovery.

Do hugs have to be physical hugs?

No. A safe hug, high-five, cuddle, warm check-in, or virtual hug can all count if that feels right to you.

Is Hug Time medical advice?

No. Hug Time is a wellness-style companion app. It is not medical advice, therapy, or a crisis service.

Where is my data stored?

Your hug goals, mood check-ins, streaks, reminders, and history are stored on your device.

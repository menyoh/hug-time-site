Hug Time Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 21, 2026

Hug Time is designed to be a small, private companion for daily connection goals. This policy explains what information the app uses and how it is handled.

Information the app stores

Hug Time stores your daily hug goal, daily hug count, streak and recovery status, mood check-ins, completed missions, reminder preferences, and recent daily hug history on your device.

Data collection

Hug Time does not collect, sell, share, or transmit your personal data to the developer. Your hug history, mood check-ins, and reminder preferences are stored locally on your device using Apple system storage.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, Hug Time asks for notification permission. Notifications are scheduled on your device and can be turned off at any time in the app or in iOS Settings.

In-app purchases

Hug Time may offer an optional streak recovery purchase. Purchases are processed by Apple through StoreKit. The developer does not receive your payment card information.

Health and wellness information

Hug Time is not a medical, mental health, or therapy app. Hug facts and prompts are informational and motivational only. If you need medical, mental health, or emergency support, contact a qualified professional or local emergency services.

Consent and safety

Hug Time encourages consent-first connection. Hugs and physical touch should always be optional, welcome, and comfortable for everyone involved.

Children

Hug Time is not intended to knowingly collect information from children. The app does not transmit personal data to the developer.

Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated as Hug Time changes. The effective date will be updated when changes are made.
